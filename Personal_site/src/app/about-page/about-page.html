About Me

I’m an indie developer and full-stack engineer with a systems-thinking mindset and hands-on
fluency in Angular, ASP.NET Core, and DevOps automation. I specialize in building scalable
web applications with responsive UI layouts, clean CRUD logic, and seamless API integration.
My journey began at Tshwane University of Technology, where I earned a National Diploma in
Computer Systems Engineering. That program taught me more than just code — it taught me how
to think in systems, connect layers, and solve problems from the ground up. After graduating, I joined
Clientele Limited as a full-stack developer, supporting live production systems across UI, backend, and database layers.
I resolved bugs, optimized performance, and collaborated with teams to improve user experience.

Later, I worked as an IT technician at Tshwane South College, troubleshooting networks, fixing lab setups,
and supporting both students and staff. I’m now expanding into cybersecurity and indie development — blending
secure systems thinking with creative freedom. I’ve earned certifications in web design, Angular/ASP.NET development,
CompTIA A+, and Git, and I’m actively building projects that reflect both technical depth and personal vision.

I live by the motto: “If it’s easy, do it easy. If it’s hard, do it hard.”
Because every challenge is an opportunity to grow.
